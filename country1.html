Montenegro
Montenegro, nestled in the Balkans, captivates visitors with its diverse landscapes. Along its Adriatic coast, pristine beaches beckon sun-seekers, while rugged mountains offer thrilling hiking adventures.
Explore medieval towns like Kotor, with its ancient city walls, or Budva, known for its charming old town and lively nightlife. Don't miss the chance to hike in Durmitor National Park, home to dramatic peaks, glacial lakes, and lush forests.
Indulge in Montenegro's culinary delights, featuring fresh seafood like grilled fish and seafood risotto. Taste hearty meats, cheeses, and olives, complemented by local wines and rakija. Montenegrin cuisine reflects its rich history and coastal influences, offering a delicious culinary journey for travelers.
